Attendance Policy

Attendance is mandatory unless PTO is taken or there is a valid medical reason. See the Attendance Policy document for full details. An Attendance Event may be created if the driver is: Tardy, has an Early Departure or is a No Call No Show. An entry is then made and points assigned.

Points are accrued based on this schedule:

Tardy = 1

Early Departure = 2

No Call No Show = 6

10 total points in one year can be reason for dismissal
